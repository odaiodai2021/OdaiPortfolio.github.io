Seesaw - 2015

سيسو

Description:

A black comedy program based on two characters who play the seesaw game to escape the pressures of social life, and the garden is the only outlet for them to talk about issues of the homeland and society that they do not dare to talk about in the street, but they practice this conversation while playing and what they talk about is happening in front of them as a dramatic scene, as if the scene It expresses their imagination and the program consists of 15 episodes
