Oqool taht El-Mijhar - 2019

عقول تحت المجهر

Description:

A documentary program that highlights Jordanian inventors and their most prominent innovations through which they obtained a patent and tried to embody these innovations on the ground through electronic and electrical devices.
Then, in the other part of the program, we monitor their suffering in marketing their products under government bureaucracy.
It is a program consisting of 30 episodes.
