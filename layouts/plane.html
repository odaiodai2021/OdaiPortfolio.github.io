The Last Mission - 2018

المهمة الأخيرة

Description:

An adventure movie that talks about the sinking of the c130 plane in the Gulf of Aqaba to turn it into a new coral reef.
Many television channels, security agencies and government authorities participated in this major event. It was a solemn event, especially when the sinking process was being prepared for over a period of 4 months.
The duration of the film is 50 minutes.
