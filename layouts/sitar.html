Description:

A social experiments program that monitors society’s view of people with disabilities and how they deal with them through a hidden camera that monitors the entire events that take place between people and the disabled.
The program relies on a dramatic scene that explains the problem they suffer from.
Then in its second part, the program reviews the opinions of people in the street about the issue, and in the last part of The program is creating a social experiment that shows the reality of society regarding the issue that people with disabilities suffer from.
The program consists of 15 episodes
