Libya Bald Al Million - 2012

ليبيا بلد المليون حافظ

Description:

In 2012, I formulated the idea of the documentary film (Libya, the Country of a Million) and researched and prepared the film, and it was filmed entirely in Libya during the Libyan revolution.
The duration of the film is 90 minutes.
It was a diverse film that talked about memorizers of the Holy Quran throughout Libya. The topics were divided in an attractive way, and we adopted the method of visual coexistence in constructing the film, its topics, and its content.
There is no home in it without a reader or memorizer of the Book of God, whose hearts soar to the sky with its words and lights until their countries become aware of them. It is Libya that is full of readers and memorizers.
